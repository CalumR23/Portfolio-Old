About Me

Hello my name is Calum Ragan and I am a front-end and back-end web developer. I discovered coding after some friends turned me onto it and I have been enamored with it ever since. Wanting to go forward with my education, I searched around and look at options when I came upon UCI's bootcamp. I was granted the opportunity to attend their Full-Stack full time course and even though it was a hard and stressful course, at the end of the day the skills I learned there have opened my eyes to what programming can actually do.

In my free time I love to game! Whether it's console or PC, I am ready to go. My favorite type of genere of video games are RPGs but I love playing FPS games with friends. When I'm not gaming, I am spending time with friends and family and usually watching Marvel movies. I'm also exploring Orange County to find the best food and my favorite cusine is Japanese food. I love to travel and have been to every continet besides Africa and Antartica.
